About Me

I'm Jordan Blum. Born and raised in Barrington, IL, a suburb of Chicago. I am currently studying Computer Science and Studio Art at Santa Clara University in Santa Clara, California.

I am an aspiring entrepreneur in the tech industry and have been pursuing all types of projects - programming and non-programming - since I was young.
I started jailbreaking my iPod Touch when I was 12 years old, and from there I have played with everything from iPods, to music production software like Ableton Live, to Photoshop and Final Cut Pro, and even created a music blog through WordPress which initially sparked my passion for programming.

I enrolled in an AP Computer Science course my junior year of high school and from there, I was hooked. Since then I have learned a bunch of languages including:
